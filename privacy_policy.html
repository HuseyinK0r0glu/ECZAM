Privacy Policy

My MedCabinet · Last updated: June 16, 2025

My MedCabinet ("the app") is a personal medicine-tracking application developed by Can Zorlu. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

The app collects and stores the following data only on your device:

  • Medicine names, dosages, and schedules you enter
  • Photos of medicine packages you choose to capture or upload
  • Dose history (taken / skipped records)

Data We Do Not Collect

  • The app has no internet connection and sends no data to any server.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting services.

Camera and Photo Library

The app requests access to your camera and photo library solely to let you attach a photo to a medicine entry. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted.

Notifications

The app requests permission to send local notifications to remind you to take your medicines. These notifications are generated entirely on-device; no notification data is sent to any server.

Data Storage

All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently. We do not have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. As no personal data is collected from any user, the app is safe for all age groups.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future app update, the revised policy will be posted here with an updated date.
